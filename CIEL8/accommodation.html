Travel, Accommodation, & Directions

TRAVEL TO BLOOMINGTON

Please note: there is no airport in Bloomington, Indiana! If you are traveling to the conference by plane, make sure your flight is booked to the Indianapolis International Airport (IND).  Some people mistakenly book tickets to the airport in Bloomington, Illinois - but that is the wrong city!! It is 3 hours away (in a different State) from Indiana Unversity, Bloomington!  Please be absolutely sure you are booking tickets to IND (Indianapolis International Airport).

You will need to arrange transportation to Bloomington from the airport in Indianapolis. There are two shuttle services that you may use: Star of America or GO Express Travel. Booking ahead of time online saves a few dollars and guarantees you a seat at a particular time, but tickets are non-refundable in the event of flight delays.

ACCOMMODATIONS IN BLOOMINGTON

A block of hotel rooms on the IU Bloomington campus have been reserved for people attending the 8th International Conference in Evolutionary Linguistics.

HOST HOTEL: INDIANA MEMORIAL UNION (IMU) 

900 East 7th Street

Bloomington, IN USA 47405

Phone: 812-856-6381

For your convenience, if you are looking for hotel accommodations, we suggest that you reserve a guestroom at the IMU. The IMU is located in the center of campus and is in easy walking distance to many of Bloomington’s downtown shops, cafes and restaurants. You can explore many of these options through the Visit Bloomington web site: www.visitbloomington.com

Rates & Reservations

In order to secure a hotel room at the Indiana Memorial Union, contact the hotel directly or reserve your room on-line. When phoning for your reservation, you must identify that you will be attending the 8th International Conference in Evolutionary Linguistics in order to obtain a room.

On-line reservation: http://imu.indiana.edu/hotel/

Group Code: LINGUISTICS2016

Phone reservations: (812) 856-6381

Room rates: current rooms rates vary from $129 - $194 depending on room type; rates are for the room itself and are not based on the number of people occupying the room. All rooms are currently subject to a 12% tax.

Reservation cut- off date: July 7, 2016

Parking: free to hotel guests

Wireless Internet: free

OFF CAMPUS ACCOMMODATIONS

Although we recommend that you stay at the IMU hotel, there are many hotel options outside of the IU Bloomington campus. Some hotels are located within walking distance; some are a short drive from campus. The Grant Street Inn and Showers Inn are also both conveniently located. Check out the Visit Bloomington web site for off campus lodging (http://www.visitbloomington.com/visitors/hotels)

Note: Attendees electing to stay at off campus hotels who wish to drive to campus and park their car on campus may do so in the two parking lots adjacent to the Indiana Memorial Union. There will be a fee for parking in these lots. Discount parking passes will be available at the registration check-in desk for conference participants. The approximate discounted parking fee is currently $12/day.
