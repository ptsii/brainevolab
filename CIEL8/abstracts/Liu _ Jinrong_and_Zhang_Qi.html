[Lahu Language is an independent language belonging to Yi Language branch of Tibeto-Burman Group under Sino-Tibetan Languagefamily. Its four-syllabic words has important value in Sino-Tibetan Languages researches. Language contact plays a critical role in the formation and development of Lahu four-syllabic words. Being used in same geographical area makes  contacts between four-syllabic words in Chinese Language and Lahu Language inevitable. The extensive and broad contacts between two languages establish the influence of genetic relationships and area typological characteristic. Through studying and analysing 5200 Lahu four-syllabic words, this paper sums up 316 four-syllabic words that contain chinese loanwords, and makes further study on its borrowing mode and word formation.]
