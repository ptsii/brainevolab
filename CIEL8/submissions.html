Submissions

Abstracts should be 500-1,000 words, and in Microsoft .doc or .docx format. The Conference will be conducted in both English and Putonghua (Mandarin Chinese), so abstracts may be in either language, or both. If your abstract is in only one language, we will assume you wish to give your presentation in that language. If your abstract is in both languages, please let us know whether you prefer to give your talk in a Putonghua section or an English section. If you are submitting in both languages, paste both abstracts into one document before uploading. Please submit no later than June 15, 2016. Formal invitations will be sent out about 2 weeks after submission.

To submit your abstract for talk or poster, please go here: CIEL8 abstract submission

If you have any questions about abstract submission or formatting, please contact us at: CIEL8.2016@gmail.com
