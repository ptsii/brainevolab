Notice No.2 from the 6th International Conference in Evolutionary Linguistics

Updated Sept. 16, 2014 Click to show

The 6th International Conference in Evolutionary Linguistics (CIEL-6), co-hosted by the Overseas Education College of Xiamen University and the Anthropological Society of China, is to be held on November 21-23, 2014 at Xiamen University.

Distinguished scholars from around the world, such as academicians William S-Y Wang, Ovid J.L.Tzeng, Paul Kay, Jin Li, Professor Thomas Schoenemann will be present and make presentations.

1. Contents and Topics for Discussion>

Conference Contents:
Probe into the evolution of language and human beings from interdisciplinary perspectives of linguistics, anthropology, archaeology, biology, genetics and neurosciences.

    Conference Theme:
  1. Language as a complex adaptive system;
  2. Language and population evolution in China;
  3. Language evolution in China and archeological evidence;
  4. Vertical and horizontal transmission of language;
  5. Language and the brain;
  6. Ancestor of Chinese Language;
  7. Other language-related research.

2. Specific Arrangements

November 21st : Check in

November 22nd to 23rd: Formal Seminars, during the period of which a performance of Fujian Nanyin, a living fossil of Chinese Opera, will be presented.

3. Cost and financing

There are no conference fees for participants. Due to the deficiency of the funding, we are unable to afford your accommodation.

Accommodation: Jinmenwan Grand Hotel, a 4-star hotel located at Dadeng Island with wonderful view of the sea, about RMB400/day/room. Please visit the hotel website for details: www.jmw-hotel.com.

Preliminary Notice for The 6th Conference in Evolutionary Linguistics

Updated Nov. 17, 2013 Click to show

The 6th International Conference in Evolutionary Linguistics (CIEL-6) will be held November 21-23, 2014 at Xiamen University. Registration will be on Nov. 21, and the conference is during Nov. 22-23. The previous annual conferences successfully held in Guangzhou, Tianjin, Shanghai, Beijing and Hong Kong have consolidated the platform for international interdisciplinary exchange on Evolutionary Linguistics research, which has promoted the development of Evolutionary Linguistics. Xiamen University is honored to hold this conference in 2014 and sincerely invites scholars interested in this field to attend the conference.

In recent years, owing to collaborative research on linguistics, anthropology, archeology, biology, genetics, neuroscience, etc., research in evolutionary linguistics has made great progress internationally and achieved substantial results. The abundant language resources, unique writing system, and complex effects of linguistic contact in China all provide vital resources for evolutionary linguistics research, and Chinese evolutionary linguistics research provides helpful references for universal language evolution research. Therefore, there is much anticipation of enhancing international interaction and interdisciplinary cooperation via this conference.

Topics of CIEL-6 include the following areas:

  1. Language as a complex adaptive system;
  2. Language and population evolution in China;
  3. Language evolution in China and archeological evidence;
  4. Vertical and horizontal transmission of language;
  5. Language and the brain;
  6. Ancestor of Chinese Language;
  7. Other language-related research.

We sincerely welcome all interested scholars to attend! Please send abstracts (500-1,000 words) to ciel2014xmu@gmail.com in doc or pdf format before July 1, 2014. Formal invitations will be sent out no later than September 20, 2014. Abstracts can be accepted both in English and Chinese. If any special symbols or fonts appear in abstracts, please submit related files. The conference will be conducted in Putonghua (Mandarin Chinese) and English.

We look forward to seeing you at Xiamen University for CIEL2014!
